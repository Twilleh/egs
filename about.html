Meet the Mariachi-Conoce al Mariachi

Take a look at some of the core musicians that form Mariachi Ruelas.

Toma un momento para conocer a los elementos del Mariachi Ruelas.

Edgar(Vihuela) has been playing since he was 7 years old. Hails from Jalisco Mexico which has a rich history of mariachis. Edgar enjoys singing songs from different artists especially when interpreting the great Vicente Fernandez.



Amado Curiel(Violin) has a vast repertoire of songs which he skillfully performs with the experience of decades under his belt.


Catarino(Trummpet), enjoys playing lead trumpet while also supplementing with some vocals.



Miguel(Guitarron), has played the Guitarron(bass guitar) for all of his career as a musician, he also enjoys interpreting songs from his favorite artist; Jose Alrfredo Jimenez
